# Narration — System Storytelling

<!-- narration:n01 -->

Modern systems are deeply interconnected. Services, queues, integrations, and state transitions form a landscape where behavior emerges from many moving parts.

<!-- narration:n02 -->

Everything works until it does not. What breaks for the user is often not where the issue starts—the failure signal appears at the surface while the trigger may lie elsewhere.

<!-- narration:n03 -->

The issue is rarely where it appears. Top-level incidents are frequently driven by deeper infrastructure and system interactions that only show up when you trace the flow.

<!-- narration:n04 -->

Root cause requires tracing the system. Observability, dependency maps, and disciplined analysis are how we move from symptom back to the real trigger.
